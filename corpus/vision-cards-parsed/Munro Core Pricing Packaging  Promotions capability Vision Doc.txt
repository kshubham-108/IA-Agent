Purpose, use and accountabilities of this document
An approved Vision is mandatory for a project to mobilise. It describes the scope and intent to be delivered by the mobilised project. The project is the prime beneficiary of the Vision in that it guides their scope and intent and provides a baseline for stakeholder conversations.
The Vision document expands on the information in the Vision card (which is the minimum documentation required for initial T-Shirt sizing of a proposal).

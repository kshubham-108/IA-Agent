PROBLEM or Opportunity Statement
The current process for fulfilling wholesale eSIM orders is manual and 
paper-based
. A wholesale partner submits an order form, which is manually processed by the SN&M team in the ABS system. The request is then sent to Thales for provisioning. Thales sends the QR code data to a third-party, Real Digital, who prints and mails the physical QR codes to the partner. 
This multi-step process is lengthy and time-consuming.
  Sometimes the Partner will choose to get a plastic SIM instead, sometimes they will connect a customer to another network that offers an easier eSIM solution such as Vodafone who’s eSIM solution is easier to use
, as is EE’s and they both use an API type conn
ection.
We need a modern, digital 
capability
 
for digital eSIM fulfilment and eSIM management 
all 13 
B2B 
wholesale partners, with a repeatable approach that can 
allow them to connect to the API as and when they are ready, so be able to get some support of they need it either from VMO2’s SIM and Numbering Team or Thales.
O2B are not involved with this VD as they are going in their own direction.  Tesco Mobile or any other MVNO are also NOT involved with this VD as they have a solution already
 for eSIM.
IOT are not included in this VD as they are on their own journey, needing a slightly different solution.

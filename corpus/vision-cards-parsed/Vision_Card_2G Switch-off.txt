Information:
Following network platforms will be switched-off and decommissioned as part of 2G switch-off (2029) project.
2G Radio BTS and BSCs that are not serving SMIP areas.
MBC and MGW - supports voice call for Non-IMS capable devices.
IP-Signalling Transfer Point – supports SS7 based signaling with Roaming Networks, National MNOs and MVNOs. (Signalling where applicable to migrate to Diameter and/or SIP signalling). Impacts of SMS delivery flows.
2G/3G HLR and authentication functions in Nokia UDB.
We anticipate above decommissioning or changes to Network Platforms will impact:
Provisioning flows - where 2G/3G provisioning steps should be removed.
Billing – CDRs from MBC to be stopped.
DCTC/Billing - SMS CDRs may move to diameter / SIP based flows; some flow and configuration and testing will be required.
Any systems where 2G cell-information is stored or maintained to be updated, and such information is to be removed.

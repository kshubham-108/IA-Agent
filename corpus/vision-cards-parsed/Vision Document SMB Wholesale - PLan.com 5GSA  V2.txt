PROBLEM or Opportunity Statement
While 5G Standalone (5GSA) services are already offered to SMB wholesale partners via the standard delivery route, Plan.com is currently unable to consume 5GSA through this route due to historic, bespoke APN routing implemented prior to the introduction of 5GSA.
As a result, although Plan.com meets the eligibility criteria for 5GSA, the service is effectively blocked by the current technical design. This initiative therefore 
seeks
 to enable service parity for Plan.com and to address regulatory obligations associated with offering equivalent services to SMB wholesale partners.
The current assumption is that delivery of this change will be funded by VMO2. The requirement to enable 5GSA arises from historic APN design decisions implemented by VMO2 and is not attributable to a Plan.com
initiated change request.
Consideration of cost responsibility for any future strategic re
alignment of Plan.com onto a fully standardised SMB architecture is outside the scope of this Vision Document.

The Vision Document serves as a key foundation for any business initiative. It outlines the core problem, objectives, expected benefits, scope, and identifies associated risks, assumptions, and dependencies. Its purpose is to provide a clear and comprehensive overview that supports accurate impact assessment and informed planning.
This document is a required input for the Ideate Pack, which is used by the Mobile & MW Portfolio pod team to evaluate the initiative across all relevant touchpoints. A well-prepared Vision Document ensures the Ideate Pack can be completed accurately and effectively.

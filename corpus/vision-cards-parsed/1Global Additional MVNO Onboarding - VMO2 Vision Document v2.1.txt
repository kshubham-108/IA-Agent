Background or context of the opportunity
VMO2 is currently onboarding the Cure MVNO via the 1 Global platform, which will establish 1 Global as a Mobile Virtual Network Enabler (MVNE) partner providing connectivity and managed integration between VMO2 and MVNOs. As part of this engagement, there is now an opportunity to assess and enable the onboarding of additional MVNOs through the same 1 Global channel, leveraging the existing integration, operational model, and commercial constructs already being established.
Describe the problem or opportunity
The current onboarding of Cure represents the first implementation of the VMO2–1 Global MVNE model. However, the ability to onboard further MVNOs through this channel has not yet been formally assessed. The opportunity is to extend this model to support additional MVNO partners with minimal incremental effort, ensuring scalability, repeatability, and commercial efficiency. This requires understanding the technical, operational, and billing impacts of introducing an additional MVNO while maintaining separation where required.
Business Objectives - what is the business trying to achieve?

  Reuse existing Cure integration, network configuration, and service models where possible 
  Ensure appropriate separation of wholesale billing and commercial arrangements per MVNO 
  Minimise incremental technical and operational effort for each new MVNO 
  Establish a scalable and repeatable MVNO onboarding framework via 1 Global
  
Enable commercial launch of a specific MVNO that will be used as pilot of this new process
What should happen in 
future
 if we address this opportunity?
If successful, VMO2 will be able to onboard multiple MVNOs through 1 Global in a streamlined and cost-efficient manner. Each MVNO will benefit from a consistent service offering and operational framework, while VMO2 retains control of billing, commercial agreements, and service 
performance. This will create a scalable MVNE capability that reduces time-to-market for new partners and supports wholesale revenue growth.
